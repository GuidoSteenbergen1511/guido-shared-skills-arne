# Default Style: Professional Editorial Workspace

Use this style when no client-specific Visual DNA exists. Inspired by Kinfolk magazine meets modern consultancy desk: warm, calm, editorially lit, intentionally composed.

## Color Palette

| Color | Hex | Role |
|-------|-----|------|
| Warm Cream | #F5F0E8 | Surfaces, backgrounds, lifted shadows |
| Amber | #D4A55A | Primary accent, props, highlights |
| Soft Sand | #E8DDD0 | Secondary warm tone, soft accents |
| Fog White | #EDEAE4 | Soft neutrals, fabric tones |
| Charcoal | #1A1A1A | Dark accents, text elements, contrast |
| Warm Gray | #B8AFA4 | Muted props, secondary surfaces |

## Subjects

- Clean workspaces, flat-lays, organized desk scenes
- Tech devices: phones, tablets, laptops (screens blurred, never readable)
- Props: coffee cups, notebooks, pens, printed reports, small plants
- Cream/warm wood surfaces
- Occasional hands interacting with objects (no full faces)

## Lighting

- Soft natural window light from one side, or overhead diffused editorial light
- Warm color temperature (Daylight or slightly warm white balance)
- Gentle shadows that add depth without drama
- No harsh directional light, no dramatic rim light

## Camera

| Shot Type | Camera | Lens | Aperture | ISO |
|-----------|--------|------|----------|-----|
| Overhead flat-lay | Hasselblad X2D | 80mm | f/4 - f/5.6 | 100-200 |
| Angled desk scene | Hasselblad X2D | 55mm | f/4 - f/5.6 | 100-200 |
| Detail / product | Hasselblad X2D | 90mm macro | f/5.6 | 100 |

Shutter speed: 1/125 for all. Focus: most of scene sharp, subtle fall-off at edges.

## Mood

- Calm, organized, intentional
- Editorial magazine quality (think Kinfolk, Cereal, Monocle)
- NOT stock photography (no forced smiles, no generic office)
- NOT Instagram lifestyle (no excessive styling, no filters)
- NOT dark/moody tech (no neon, no dark backgrounds)

## Color Grade: "Warm Editorial"

- Shadows: lifted toward cream (#F5F0E8), never true black
- Midtones: warm amber shift, skin tones stay natural
- Highlights: soft sand (#E8DDD0), never blown white
- Non-warm tones: desaturated (blues, greens pushed toward neutral gray)
- Overall: warm, soft contrast, magazine-quality tonal range

## Prompt Modifier Block

Copy-paste these into any prompt built with this style:

**Color/mood string:**
```
The colour scheme is warm cream and soft amber, with a calm editorial tone.
```

**Lighting string:**
```
Lighting is soft natural window light from camera-left creating gentle wrap-around illumination on a cream surface, warm color temperature throughout.
```

**Color grade string:**
```
The image has been post-processed with a Warm Editorial grade -- cream-toned lifted shadows, warm amber midtones, soft sand highlights, desaturated non-warm tones, magazine-quality tonal range.
```

**Setting string:**
```
The scene is a bright, warm editorial workspace with cream surfaces and natural light, and the subject is [SUBJECT].
```

## What Works

- Amber or warm-toned accent props
- Cream/warm wood surfaces
- Coffee in ceramic cups (warm tones)
- Printed materials with minimal, neutral accents
- Overhead flat-lay compositions with intentional negative space
- Natural linen or cotton textures in frame

## What Doesn't Work

- Mountains, outdoor adventure scenes (wrong context for this style)
- Dark moody environments (contradicts warm editorial mood)
- Neon tech aesthetics (wrong palette entirely)
- Busy cluttered scenes (editorial = intentional, curated)
- Cool blue/teal color temperatures (fights the warm palette)
- People as primary subjects (this style is about objects and spaces)
- Readable text on screens (always blur or abstract)
