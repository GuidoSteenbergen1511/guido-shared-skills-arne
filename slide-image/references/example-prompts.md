# Example Prompts

5 detailed prompts with generation settings and notes on results.

## Prompt 1: Workshop Collaboration (Pentland)

**Concept:** Team of professionals collaborating around a large screen displaying AI workflows

**Prompt:**
```
A diverse group of four professionals gathered around a large digital display showing workflow diagrams, actively discussing and pointing at elements on screen. They wear smart casual business attire in navy and neutral tones. One person is standing, gesturing toward the screen, while others lean in with engaged expressions. The background is a modern innovation lab with deep navy walls and warm ambient lighting. The photo style is Editorial, taken as a medium shot with a Canon EOS R5 camera and a 35mm lens focal length. The aperture is set to f/2.8, the shutter speed is 1/125, and the ISO is 400. The white balance is set to Tungsten. The focus is on the standing presenter with gentle bokeh on the background and edges of the group. The colour scheme is deep navy and warm amber, with a cinematic tone. Lighting is practical overhead strips in cool white mixed with warm golden accent from the left, creating depth and warmth on faces. The scene is Indoor modern corporate innovation space, and the subject is a professional team. The composition follows Triangular technique, taken from slightly below eye level. The image has been post-processed with a Cinematic Teal-Orange grade — cool navy shadows, warm amber midtones, rich natural skin tones.
```

**Settings:** model: nano-banana-pro-preview, aspectRatio: 16:9, imageSize: 2K, referenceMode: style, referenceStrength: 0.7

**What worked:** Strong depth, warm tones matched Pentland palette, natural interaction pose. The Tungsten white balance combined with the teal-orange grade created the right navy-amber mood.

**What to avoid:** Don't add too many people — 3-4 is ideal. More than 5 creates chaos.

## Prompt 2: AI Tools Flat-Lay (Default Style)

**Concept:** Overhead flat-lay workspace with tablet, orange sticky notes, coffee on cream surface

**Prompt:**
```
An overhead flat-lay of a clean workspace on a warm cream surface. A tablet displays a blurred dashboard with orange and peach colored charts. Three orange sticky notes are arranged neatly beside the tablet, one with a small hand-drawn flowchart. A ceramic cup of espresso sits in the lower right. A printed report with orange header accents and a fine-tip pen rest nearby. The photo style is Editorial, taken as an overhead shot with a Hasselblad X2D camera and a 80mm lens focal length. The aperture is set to f/5.6, the shutter speed is 1/125, and the ISO is 100. The white balance is set to Daylight. The focus is sharp across the entire scene with subtle fall-off at the very edges. The colour scheme is warm cream and soft orange, with a calm editorial tone. Lighting is soft natural window light from camera-left creating gentle wrap-around illumination on a cream surface, warm color temperature throughout. The scene is a bright, warm editorial workspace with cream surfaces and natural light, and the subject is workspace objects. The composition follows Central Grouping technique with intentional negative space on the left, taken from directly overhead. The image has been post-processed with a Warm Editorial grade -- cream-toned lifted shadows, warm orange midtones, soft peach highlights, desaturated non-warm tones, magazine-quality tonal range.
```

**Settings:** model: nano-banana-pro-preview, aspectRatio: 16:9, imageSize: 2K

**What worked:** Orange sticky notes match warm amber tones. Cream surface grounds the warm palette. Blurred dashboard avoids text rendering issues.

**What to avoid:** Don't make screens readable. Don't add too many props -- editorial means intentional, curated.

## Prompt 3: Data Strategy Session (Generic Business)

**Concept:** Individual professional reviewing data on a laptop, thoughtful expression

**Prompt:**
```
A professional woman in her 30s sitting at a minimalist desk, reviewing data visualizations on a laptop screen. She has a thoughtful expression, one hand on her chin, the other on the trackpad. A cup of coffee sits nearby. The desk is clean with a notebook and pen. The background is a bright, modern office with floor-to-ceiling windows showing a blurred cityscape. The photo style is Commercial editorial, taken as a medium close-up shot with a Canon EOS R5 camera and a 85mm lens focal length. The aperture is set to f/2.0, the shutter speed is 1/200, and the ISO is 200. The white balance is set to Daylight. The focus is on the woman's face and hands, with the laptop screen slightly soft and the background windows beautifully blurred. The colour scheme is cool whites and warm naturals, with a professional warm tone. Lighting is large soft window light from camera-left creating gentle wrap-around illumination, with a subtle fill bounce from the right. The scene is Indoor modern office, and the subject is a professional woman. The composition follows Rule of Thirds technique, taken from eye level. The image has been post-processed with a Corporate Warm grade — lifted shadows, clean whites, warm but not orange skin tones.
```

**What worked:** Natural, non-posed feel. Window light gave depth without drama.

**What to avoid:** Don't specify laptop brand or specific screen content — it often renders badly.

## Prompt 4: Desk Scene with Laptop (Default Style)

**Concept:** Angled desk scene with laptop, orange notebook, warm window light

**Prompt:**
```
A 30-degree angle view of a clean desk with a laptop showing a blurred colorful interface. An orange linen notebook sits closed beside the laptop, a fine black pen resting on top. A small potted succulent in a cream ceramic pot adds life to the scene. The desk surface is warm light wood. A window on the right casts soft natural light across the scene, creating gentle shadows on the left side of the objects. The background is softly blurred showing a bright, airy room. The photo style is Editorial, taken as a medium shot with a Hasselblad X2D camera and a 55mm lens focal length. The aperture is set to f/4, the shutter speed is 1/125, and the ISO is 200. The white balance is set to Daylight. The focus is on the notebook and laptop with subtle background blur. The colour scheme is warm cream and soft orange, with a calm editorial tone. Lighting is soft natural window light from camera-right creating gentle directional illumination across a warm wood surface, warm color temperature throughout. The scene is a bright, warm editorial workspace with warm wood desk and natural light, and the subject is a laptop and accessories. The composition follows Rule of Thirds technique with the laptop at the left third and notebook at the right, taken from 30-degree angle. The image has been post-processed with a Warm Editorial grade -- cream-toned lifted shadows, warm orange midtones, soft peach highlights, desaturated non-warm tones, magazine-quality tonal range.
```

**Settings:** model: nano-banana-pro-preview, aspectRatio: 16:9, imageSize: 2K

**What worked:** Window light from one side creates depth and warmth. Orange notebook is a strong brand accent without being forced. Warm wood surface complements the cream palette.

**What to avoid:** Don't make the room too busy behind the desk. Keep background soft and airy.

## Prompt 5: Executive Presentation Moment

**Concept:** A speaker presenting to a small executive audience

**Prompt:**
```
A confident male executive presenting to a small group of 5 seated colleagues in a boardroom. He stands beside a large screen showing a simple chart with upward trend. The audience members have engaged, attentive expressions. The room has dark wood paneling and modern furniture. The photo style is Editorial corporate, taken as a medium-wide shot with a Canon EOS R5 camera and a 50mm lens focal length. The aperture is set to f/2.8, the shutter speed is 1/125, and the ISO is 800. The white balance is set to Tungsten. The focus is on the presenter with the audience softly visible, the screen slightly blurred. The colour scheme is warm wood tones with cool screen glow, with a professional cinematic tone. Lighting is overhead recessed lighting providing base illumination, the presentation screen casting cool blue-white on the presenter's face, warm practical lights on the back wall creating depth. The scene is Indoor executive boardroom, and the subject is a business presenter. The composition follows Rule of Thirds technique with the presenter at the left third, taken from audience perspective slightly right. The image has been post-processed with a Cinematic Boardroom grade — rich warm midtones, deep shadows in corners, slight vignette drawing focus to the presenter.
```

**What worked:** Mixed lighting (warm room + cool screen) created visual interest. Audience blur gave depth.

**What to avoid:** Don't try to make text on the presentation screen readable — it always looks wrong. Keep it abstract shapes/charts.
